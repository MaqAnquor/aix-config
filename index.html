FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is AI Chat Exporter? +

A free browser extension that saves your AI chat conversations as clean, well-formatted Markdown. Open a chat, click the icon, and copy or download the whole thing — with headings, lists, tables, code blocks and even the model's reasoning preserved.

Which AI platforms are supported? +

12+ today: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Grok, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, Mistral (Le Chat), Qwen, z.ai / GLM, Kimi and Duck.ai — with more added regularly.

Is it free? +

Yes — exporting to Markdown and copying to your clipboard is free forever, with no account. An optional Pro plan — monthly, annual, or a one-time lifetime license — adds PDF and Word export, Notion and Obsidian sync, Google Drive backup and bulk export. You can also support development with a one-time tip.

Is my data private? +

Completely. Everything runs locally in your browser — there is no server, no account and no tracking, and we collect zero conversation data. The only network request is a small public config file that helps the exporter keep working when sites change; it contains none of your information.

Can I export only part of a conversation? +

Yes. Open "Select messages" in the popup and tick exactly the turns you want — or export the whole conversation in one click.

Does it capture the model's reasoning? +

When a platform exposes the model's "thinking", you can include it in your export with a single toggle — or leave it out. It's off by default.

Does it work on Edge and Firefox? +

It works on Chrome and Microsoft Edge today. A Firefox version is on the roadmap.
